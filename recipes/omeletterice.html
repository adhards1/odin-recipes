Omelette Rice Recipe

Enjoy thai omelette rice at home just like they do on plastic stools in Bangkok!

Ingredients
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup of rice
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Golden Mountain Seasoning
Steps
  1. Put rice on in rice cooker or over stove prior to cooking eggs
  2. Heat sesame oil on high in frying pan or wok
  3. Whisk eggs and pour into pan after pan is on very high heat
  4. Cook egg for 30 seconds before dropping over rice on plate
  5. Add chilli vinegar, sesame oil, and golden mountain seasoning to taste
  6. Finish with green onion and toasted sesame seeds
