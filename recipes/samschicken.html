Sam's Chicken Recipe

Ingredients
  • Full chicken
  • Lemon
  • Rosemary
  • Leaks
  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, Pepper, Seasoning
  1. Prepare oven at 450 degrees farenheight
  2. Stuff chicken with lemon, rosemary and cover with salt, pepper, olive oil.
  3. Cut leaks and potatoes into one inch pieces and place at bottom of pan, cover in salt, pepper, and olive oil
  4. Tie chicken legs closed, place on rack and place rack over veggies in pan.
  5. Cook chicken and veggies in oven for 1 and 1/2 hours, checking frequently and spraying with salt water if unable to salt overnight prior to cooking
  6. Once fully cooked set out to rest prior to carving and serving
